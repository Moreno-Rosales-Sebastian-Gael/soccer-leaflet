C. International Sports

When did people start doing it?
Soccer was introduced to Mexico in the late 19th century. The first games were organized by English miners in cities like Pachuca.

Why did it become popular?
It became incredibly popular because it requires minimal equipment. Over time, it grew into a fundamental trait of our cultural identity.

Basic Rules & Equipment:
Played with 11 players per team on a grass field. The fundamental rule is simple: no hands allowed (except for the goalkeeper)! All you need to play is a ball and two goals.
